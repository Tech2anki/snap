Vivo Smartphones and Significance

Vivo Communication Technology Co. Ltd. Popularly known as Vivo is primarily a Chinese company that designs and assembles smartphones and its accessories. It is a part of BBK Electronics along with other smartphone manufacturers Oppo, OnePlus and Realme. It has its research and development centres in Nanjing, Shenzhen and even in San Diego (USA). From the outset, the Chinese company has shown the courage to become a perfectionist and tried hard to reach new heights. Vivo is the first smartphone manufacturing company that introduced a pop-up selfie camera and an in-display fingerprint scanner. The leading smartphone, as well as the electronic brand, aims at offering good quality products as well as services to its customers and appeals mainly to the youth with its models like Vivo Z1x, Vivo V17 Pro, Vivo V15 Pro and Vivo Z1 Pro.
